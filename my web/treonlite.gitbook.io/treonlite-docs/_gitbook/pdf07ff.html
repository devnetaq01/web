About

TREON-Lite develops meme token projects to raise awareness of malicious practices increasingly enabled by admin privileges in the crypto space—such as rug pulls, honeypots, and fake liquidity. The project aims to provide transparent, ethical, and governance-free alternatives meme tokenes to such behaviors. It offers no investment promise and has no affiliation with any individual or brand.

PROJECTS

⚠️ Official Notice

This GitBook has been prepared by the TREON-Lite team to highlight the risks of admin privileges in smart contracts, raise awareness about these risks, and advocate for decentralized, ownerless token structures.

Our first project, TrumpElon, was created as a humorous response to such abuses. Its features include:

🔥 Liquidity LP NFTs were sent to the dead address and permanently burned. 🧨 All admin privileges were renounced; ownership has been fully removed. ❌ It includes no risky functions such as mint, pause, blacklist, or fee-switch.

TrumpElon is not an investment vehicle—it is a statement: Meme tokens can exist without centralized control.

📌 Future projects that share this vision will be announced over time via TREON-Lite’s social channels.

⚠️ All information in this GitBook should be independently verified. Contract addresses, ownership status, and function permissions must always be checked using Etherscan or other on-chain tools.

📌 The content here is for informational purposes only and does not constitute investment advice. This warning applies not only to TREON-Lite but to any project you may encounter:

❗“It’s written in the documents” is not enough—always do your own research.
